Atmospheric Depth

By strictly adhering to the No-Line Rule, we use a 40px diffuse shadow to separate notifications from the background canvas. This creates an organic elevation that feels integrated rather than appended.

Functional Accents

A singular 4px leading bar provides immediate semantic recognition without overwhelming the minimalist aesthetic. High-contrast labels ensure accessibility while maintaining the Seoul Minimalist tone.
